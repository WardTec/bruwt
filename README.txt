"Bruwt"

University of Washington (Tacoma) course regisration "brute-forcer".

------------------------

ABOUT

This script attempts to register for a course at the University of Washington Tacoma repeatedly.

It will not stop attempting to register until the course is added.

-------------------------

DISCLAIMER

The author of this program holds no responsibility for what this script may do to your UW account.

Use at your own risk.

------------------------

EXECUTION

python.exe bruwt.py

or

chmod +x bruwt.py
./bruwt.py

The script will prompt for all the required information and then begin the registration process over, and over, and over again.
